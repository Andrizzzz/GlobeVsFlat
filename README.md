## 🔧 Features Explained

- **Distance Calculations**  
  GlobeVsFlat calculates straight-line distances as the crow flies on both Earth models. This lets you see how distances change depending on the shape of the Earth.  

- **Flight Paths**  
  The tool draws flight routes between airports or cities. You can see curved flight paths on the globe, and how these change on a flat map.  

- **Map Projections**  
  GlobeVsFlat uses several map projection techniques, including azimuthal projection. These change how the round Earth appears as a flat map. You can compare them.  

- **Qibla Direction**  
  The app helps you find the correct direction to the Kaaba from any location, using geodesic calculations on the globe and flat Earth.  

- **Interactive Map Controls**  
  Pan, zoom, and rotate the view with mouse controls. This makes exploring easy and intuitive.

## 🗺️ How the Earth Models Work

**Spherical Earth:**  
This is the real shape of the Earth, a sphere. Distances follow curved lines called great circles. Flights often take curved paths here.

**Flat Earth:**  
This model shows Earth as a flat disk. Distances and directions are different here. GlobeVsFlat helps you see where this model produces errors or distortions.

The app combines geography, navigation, and math to show these differences clearly.
